Why clients choose us

Our teams strive to add value to clients' organizations over an intensive 6 – 12 month partnership

Teams frequently update clients on the revisions and progress of their work

At the end of each semester, we present clients with tangible deliverables, outlining our research, past progress, and options for the future

We have a strong track record to demonstrate our success


Why students want to join

MCG is an educational stepping stone on the path to a successful consulting career

We organize networking sessions with alums at top consulting firms, as well as educational workshops

Our members are held accountable for the professionalism of their work

As members of our club graduate each year, the top 30% of applicants are admitted into the organization after an interview process
